Personality Quiz

Pokemon Mystery Dungeon Red and Blue Rescue Team Super Condensed Personality Quiz

The Personality Quiz is the nine question quiz that determines the Pokémon that the player will play as throughout each Mystery Dungeon game. The test is always taken at the start of every game. It consists of eight personality questions, but this will be a much shorter quiz for this assignment. Based on the users response the player is assigned a Pokémon.

Possible starters for this Pokémon personality quiz are:

  • Charmander
  • Bulbasaur
  • Squirtle
  • Pikachu

Remember three things when taking this quiz:

  1. Be honest
  2. Have fun
  3. It doesn't really matter
